Holistic Skill Balance

By grouping the specific "Super Skills" into broader developmental categories, we can visualize the "shape" of the child's growth. The program emphasizes Cognitive Processing and Social/Emotional Intelligence heavily, ensuring that physical activity is purposeful and connected to brain development.

Cognitive Processing

Includes Memory, Planning, Logic, and Math Prep. Essential for school readiness.

Emotional Intelligence

Includes Empathy, Self-Regulation, and identifying feelings.

Sensory & Motor

Includes Proprioception, Balance, Fine/Gross Motor skills.

Physical Skill Breakdown

The "Strong Body" section isn't just about running. It targets specific physical literacies. Gross Motor coordination leads the way, followed closely by Stamina and Balance.

Heart & Connection

A critical balance exists between understanding oneself (Internal) and connecting with others (External). The data shows a near-perfect 50/50 split between Emotional Literacy and Social Connection.
